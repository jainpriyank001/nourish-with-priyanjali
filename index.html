About Priyanjali

Nutrition rooted in science,
shaped around you.

Priyanjali is a New Delhi-based dietitian who blends biotechnology research with hands-on nutrition practice. She holds an M.Tech in Biotechnology from NIET and a B.Tech in Biotechnology from Amity University — a background that gives her an unusually rigorous lens on metabolism and how the body actually responds to food.

She currently works as a Clinical Dietitian at Fitelo, and previously spent a year as a Clinical Nutritionist at GoDiet, where she crafted bespoke plans for clients managing weight, PCOS, thyroid, diabetes, and gut-health concerns. Her approach is grounded in evidence, never restriction — because real change comes from plans you can live with, not from willpower alone.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Is the first session really free?

Yes — the 20-minute discovery call is completely free with no obligation. It's a chance for us to see if we're the right fit before you commit to anything.

Do you offer online consultations?

Absolutely. All sessions are held over video call, so you can join from anywhere in the world. Plans, check-ins, and resources are shared digitally.

How long until I see results?

Most clients notice better energy and digestion within 2–3 weeks. Visible changes in weight, bloodwork, or symptoms usually show up within 6–12 weeks — the goal is always sustainable, not overnight.

Do you provide meal plans?

Yes, every paid program includes a personalized meal plan tailored to your preferences, schedule, and cultural context — along with recipes, swaps, and grocery guidance.

Is this suitable for vegetarians and vegans?

Completely. A large portion of clients are vegetarian or vegan, and plans are built to hit every nutrient target from plant-forward ingredients you already cook with.

What's your cancellation policy?

Sessions can be rescheduled free of charge with 24 hours' notice. Programs come with a clear refund policy shared before you sign up — transparency is the standard.
